ABOUT

Kathakali is the first south Indian restaurant in Taranaki situated at the heart of Houston… We serve south Indian delicacies in a cool and quiet ambiance.

Our specialty is different kerala seafood, meen curry are famous enough but our chefs are more creative

They can astonish you with a variety of dishes in veg and non veg, some of the dishes are named as kerala tomato fish, tuna pasta, fish masala curry, tuna apple salad and a lot more to name…

Indian food is famous for its richness, our dum biryanis and curries are really delightful, the spicy aroma itself is appetizing, especially south Indian food is lot more spicy and enticing. Welcome all out there to come, enjoy and experience the delicacies of Indian cuisine.
